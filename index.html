NNToolChain 中文文档¶

NNToolChain 是用于编译和部署深度学习模型到Sophon TPU的工具(https://sophon.ai), 包含

  1. Caffe/TensorFlow/MXNet/PyTorch 的前端parser
  2. 计算图优化的编译器及运行时
  3. Layer-based API: bmdnn/bmcv (similar to cuDNN for NVIDIA GPU);
  4. Tensor/Operator-based API: bmlang (writing custom op);
